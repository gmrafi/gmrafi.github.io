About Me

I am an undergraduate student at the Army Institute of Business Administration (Army IBA), Sylhet—an affiliated institute of Bangladesh University of Professionals (BUP). Currently working as Web Developer & Tech Associate at Sylhet Sustainable Development Research Firm (SSDRF), I am passionate about bridging business and technology through innovative research and development.

My recent achievements include participating in the 2nd International Case Conference on Business and Management (ICCBM) 2025 at BRAC University with accepted research on AI-driven nano loans, achieving Regional 1st Runner-Up in NASA's Space Apps Challenge 2025 with OrbitBiZ, and volunteering at WordCamp Dhaka 2025. I have published research in conferences like ICCTASS 2025 and ICFLEW 2025, plus a journal article in the International Journal of Sustainability and Multidisciplinary Research. As a dedicated WordPress web designer and open-source contributor, I serve 4 hours weekly with WordPress Polyglots and Training teams, having attended WordCamp Sylhet 2024 and WordCamp Europe 2024. Writing and participating in hackathons remain passions that help me apply strategic thinking to real-world business and tech challenges.
