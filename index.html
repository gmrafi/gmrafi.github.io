About Me

I am an undergraduate student at the Army Institute of Business Administration (Army IBA), Sylhet—an affiliated institute of Bangladesh University of Professionals (BUP). Currently working as Web Developer & Tech Associate at Sylhet Sustainable Development Research Firm (SSDRF), I am passionate about exploring opportunities in the business and technology landscape.

Beyond academics, I am a dedicated WordPress web designer and an active participant in the open-source community. I contribute 4 hours per week to WordPress Polyglots and Training teams, attended WordCamp Sylhet 2024 and WordCamp Europe 2024 (Contributor Days). Writing holds a special place in my heart, and I enjoy expressing my thoughts through words—it's not just a skill, it's a true passion.

I regularly participate in hackathons and technological events, constantly seeking to expand my knowledge and apply it to real-world challenges. My strategic thinking and analytical skills enable me to bridge business principles with technological innovation.
